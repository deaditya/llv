About

LLV Partners Korea is a professional investment consulting, project development and management firm

We are specialized in alternative investment, project development & management in the fields of energy infrastructure and real estate. We closely work with both global and local institutional investors and private equity under solid partnerships and networks with them.

From project development & management, investment acquisition, to assets management with exit strategies, our specialists guarantee successful completion and robust returns to our clients while risks and uncertainty are rigorously hedged.

We are reliable and trusted investment partners for Korean energy infrastructure, which now enjoys a strong growth under favorable regulatory and policy environment enabled by the Central and Local Governments.
